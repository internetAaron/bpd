Nicole

Nicole graduated from Edgewood High School in 2003. She attended Ball State University where she received her Bachelor of Science Degree in Dance and Theatre Performance in 2007. Nicole first started working at Bloomington Pediatric Dentistry in the summer of 2004, after completing her first year of college. She performed tasks such as sterilization, setting up work stations, and helping out in any area that was needed. Nicole returned to Bloomington Pediatric Dentistry in 2008 working as a Sterilization Tech/ Dental Assistant. Almost a year later, Nicole expressed interest in working on the business side of the practice. She became a front office receptionist focusing on how to make the practice more efficient. In 2011 Nicole became Office Manager of Bloomington Pediatric Dentistry. She feels very blessed to be in this position and to work with such amazing people. Nicole is CPR certified and is working towards becoming DALE certified as Office Manager. Nicole is married to Aaron and has two beautiful children Ava and Cooper. She enjoys spending time with family and friends, shopping, having dance parties with her kids, running, and working out at The C Zone.
