Luz

Luz was born in Mexico City and is a native speaker of Spanish. She moved to Bloomington 10 years ago and is married to Edwardo. They have three sons: Luis, Cesar, and Sebastian that keep her very busy. Luz attended Vincennes University Jasper Campus and received a Certificate in Dental Assisting in 2010. Luz is CPR certified and has her license in radiography. Luz started her career at Bloomington Pediatric Dentistry in 2012. Immediately Luz expressed an interest in providing education classes for the Latino community in the Bloomington area and has been working on translating our Smile Baby Class and other educational materials into Spanish. Luz loves outdoor activities, cooking, volunteering, and spending time with her family.
