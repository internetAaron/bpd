Our Office

Bloomington Pediatric Dentistry was designed to be a playground for a child’s imagination - a kind, gentle, creative environment that looks and feels nothing like a typical dental office.

Our walls come to life with the story of a brave boy named Jack and his wonderful adventures.

Through his good deeds and bravery, Jack convinced the Giant of Floss and Brush Castle to share his wonderful kingdom with other brave boys and girls.
