Cavity Free Club

We are proud to announce a program that we have in place for our patients that come in for dental cleanings and check-ups! It is our Cavity Free Club!

We want our patients to know how proud we are of them for their awesome efforts at home in between dental visits. When they have a great check-up and are cavity free they will be entered into a monthly drawing to win a fantastic prize.

Every month we will post the winner on our website and Facebook with a parents permission.
