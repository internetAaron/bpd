Insurance

Bloomington Pediatric Dentistry is proudly in network with Delta Dental Premier and Health Resources. We also accept Indiana Medicaid. However, we accept all dental insurace plans and make every effort to work with each and every company. If you have questions about how your insurance will work with Bloomington Pediatric Dentistry, please call our office and ask to speak with our insurance and billing coordinator.

It is important to remember that the pediatric dentists of Bloomington Pediatric Dentistry follow the guidelines set forth by the American Academy of Pediatric Dentistry when it comes to treatment recommendations for your child. If your insurance differs from what the American Academy of Pediatric Dentistry guidelines state, you should call your insurance company and ask why they don't follow the highest standards of care for your child. If you would like to review the American Academy of Pediatric Dentistry guidelines you may visit www.aapd.org.
