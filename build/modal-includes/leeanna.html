Leeanna

Leeanna started her career in Dental Assisting after graduating from Professional Careers Institute in 1994. Leeanna has been employed at Bloomington Pediatric Dentistry as an Expanded Functions Dental Assistant since 2010. She is CPR certified and licensed in radiography. Leeanna is our OSHA Compliance Coordinator ensuring that our office and employees remain OSHA compliant daily. Leeanna enjoys living in the country. She loves to take walks in the woods and sell vegetables at the Bloomington market. Leeanna is the mother of two and enjoys spending most of her time playing with her kids, teaching them about nature, and dancing. Sometimes, on a nice day, you will find her on the front porch in a rocking chair with a good book.
