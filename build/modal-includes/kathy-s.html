Kathy S.

Kathy grew up on Lima, Ohio. She is married to David and they have three grown children- Julie, Karyn, and Eric. Kathy has been a dental hygienist for more than 30 years and has worked specifically with children for more than 10 years. She graduated from Ohio State with a degree in Dental Hygiene. Kathy has been an employee of Bloomington pediatric Dentistry since 2003. Kathy is CPR certified. She enjoys sewing, reading, and volunteering at the Crisis Pregnancy Center.
