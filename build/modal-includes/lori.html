Lori

Lori graduated from Indiana University in 1996 earning a Bachelor of Science Degree in Telecommunications. She joined the staff of Bloomington Pediatric Dentistry in 2011 as a Patient Care Coordinator. She feels blessed to be a part of a committed and caring team. She is dedicated to continuous learning so that she may be able to provide the best service to our young patients and their parents. Lori is CPR certified. Outside of work, Lori spends the majority of her time with her husband of sixteen years and their two daughters. Together they enjoy going to movies, county fairs, and high school sports.
