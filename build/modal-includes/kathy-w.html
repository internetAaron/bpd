Kathy W.

Kathy grew up in northern Indiana on Lake Michigan. She is married to Eric and they have three boys. Kathy spent two years at Indiana University Bloomington and two years at Indiana University School of Dentistry in Indianapolis earning an Associate Degree of Applied Science in 1995. Kathy first started at Bloomington Pediatric Dentistry as a temporary hygienist and became an employee in 2010. Kathy is CPR certified. She enjoys vacationing, hanging out with her family and friends, running, rollerblading, and playing Just Dance on the Wii!
