Jana

Jana was born in Bloomington, Indiana. She is married to Josh and they have 2 beautiful children. Jana graduated from Bloomington High School South in 2004. She attended the University of Southern Indiana and received a Bachelor of Science Degree in Dental Hygiene and an Associate of Science Degree in Dental Hygiene and Dental Assisting. Jana is CPR certified. Jana started her career at Bloomington Pediatric Dentistry in 2008. Being a mother, Jana expressed interest in teaching our community about oral hygiene. In 2012 Jana became our Prenatal Oral Health Education Coordinator. Jana recently helped launch our Smile Baby! Born to be Cavity Free community education program for parents and expectant parents. Jana loves spending time with family and friends, doing photography, and participating in outdoor activities.
